# Verified facts for loanapp5 content (Kenya loans)

Operator-supplied research pack, checked against lender product pages, tariff guides,
app-store disclosures, CBK publications and Kenya's digital-credit regulations.
**Research date: 23 June 2026.** Underscore-prefixed so Next.js never serves it.

Do NOT launch a fresh fact-checking exercise. Only re-verify a figure if the source
page is now unavailable or shows materially different terms. Where pricing is
personalised, use: "Your final rate, fees, limit and repayment amount will be shown
in the lender's offer or Key Facts Statement before you accept the loan."

## OKash — DO NOT present as a current Kenyan lender
No currently licensed Kenyan OKash in the April 2026 CBK DCP directory. The live
"OKash" app is a Nigerian product (naira, 91-365-day terms) — never publish its terms
as Kenyan. Historical KE figures only: ~1%/day, up to 14 days, repeat limit up to
KSh500,000. Warn against upfront verification/registration fee scams (e.g. KSh95).

## Tala Kenya
Daily interest 0.3%-0.6% (published APR 109.5%-219%). Max term 120 days. Max loan
KSh50,000. No processing fee. Excise duty 20% of interest.
Example: KSh10,000 at 0.3%/day for 30 days = KSh900 interest + KSh180 excise = repay KSh11,080.

## Branch Kenya
Monthly interest 2%-18% (APR ~20%-211%). Term 62 days-12 months. Loan ~KSh500-300,000.
Personalised. DO NOT say categorically "no processing fee" — disclosures inconsistent
(one says none, another ~6%); any fee shows in the borrower's in-app offer.

## Zenka Kenya
One-off interest charge 2.45%-39% of principal. Term up to 91 days. Loan KSh500-200,000.
Processing fee KSh0. Licensed (Zenka Digital Ltd in CBK directory). Do not headline the
highest annualised APR.

## KCB M-Pesa
One-month loan fee 7.35%; +20% excise (1.47%) = ~8.82% all-in. Term one month.
Min ~KSh1,000, advertised max up to KSh1M (individually scored).
SAVINGS: replace old ~6.3%/yr — KCB now advertises >8.5%/yr on fixed/target savings.

## Timiza (Absa)
Standard 30-day: access fee 8.75% + 20% excise = 10.5% effective upfront deduction.
Max KSh150,000. Late penalty 10%. Example KSh10,000: KSh875 fee + KSh175 excise =
KSh1,050 deducted, ~KSh8,950 reaches wallet (wallet-to-M-Pesa transfer may cost extra).
Separate longer-term product (do NOT merge): 8% initial access fee + excise + 5%/month
of principal due + KSh42/month insurance.

## Equity Eazzy Loan
Personalised: Equity Bank Reference Rate + customer margin. Appraisal/credit-eval charge
up to 5% (+tax). Max term 24 months. Max loan KSh3M. DO NOT publish one fixed rate.

## Hustler Fund
Personal: 8%/yr prorated daily, initial 14-day term, KSh100-50,000. 95% to wallet, 5%
to savings (NOT a fee). Overdue rate 9.5%/yr after term. Group loan up to KSh1M / ~30
days. Bridge loan up to 3x assessed limit / 30 days / 8%/yr. Do NOT publish a fixed
national daily allocation (those are performance stats, not borrower limits).

## CRB small-default threshold
Licensed DCP may NOT submit negative CRB info where outstanding <= KSh1,000.
Lender must notify borrower before listing (normally >= 30 days). Specific to licensed
DCPs — not automatically every bank/SACCO/MFI. DELETE "very small defaults" vagueness
and the KSh5M figure (that was a temporary pandemic-era suspension, no longer operative).

## Checking a lender's licence
April 2026 CBK directory listed 227 licensed DCPs. Match app/brand name AND legal
company name (+ website, phone, email, address) against the directory. Bank-backed
products (KCB M-Pesa, Timiza, Equity Eazzy) may NOT appear in the DCP directory because
banks/MFBs are regulated separately.

## Hidden / easily-missed fees (use real examples)
- Excise duty 20%: Tala (on interest), KCB (on fee), Timiza (on access fee).
- Upfront deduction: Timiza KSh10,000 -> ~KSh8,950 spendable.
- Insurance/appraisal: Equity Boostika KSh10,000/30d ~ appraisal 5% (KSh500) + excise
  (KSh100) + insurance 1% (KSh100) + interest ~KSh148 = ~KSh848 total (Boostika only).
- Wallet-transfer fees: Timiza (wallet to M-Pesa).
- Processing fees: Branch (confirm in offer).
- Late charges: Timiza 10%; Hustler Fund 8% -> 9.5%/yr after term.

## Fuliza fees
One-off access fee 1%. Daily maintenance fee from next day. Excise on relevant fees.
Bands (base / with excise): 1-100 = 0/0; 101-500 = 2.50/3.00; 501-1,000 = 5.00/6.00;
1,001-1,500 = 18.00/21.60; 1,501-2,500 = 20.00/24.00; 2,501-70,000 = 25.00/30.00.
Three-day waiver on daily fee for balances <= KSh1,000 (1% access fee still applies).
